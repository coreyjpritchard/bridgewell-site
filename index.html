The process

From scan to sorted, in four steps

No mystery, no endless discovery phase. You always know which step you're on and what it costs.

STEP 01

Scan

You give us your URL. We run the automated WCAG 2.2 checks and flag every issue our tooling can see.

STEP 02

Snapshot

A human reads the results and writes the plain-English report — the honest score, the priorities, the real impact.

STEP 03

Fix or rebuild

Small list? We patch it. Foundations shot? We rebuild properly. Either way you get a fixed quote first.

STEP 04

Care

Optional ongoing plan with a quarterly re-scan, so the site stays accessible as you add new content.
